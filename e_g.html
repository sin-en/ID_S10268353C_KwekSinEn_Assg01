About them

They are a 4th gen K-Pop boy band that was formed during a 2017 reality show and debuted in the entertainment company called JYPE. They have eight members, Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Han Jisung, HyunJin, Felix, Seungmin and I.N. They also had nine members before one of them, Woojin, left due to personal reasons in 2019, only 2 years after their debut. They mainly produce their own music with the three songwriters, Bang Chan, Changbin and Han Jisung going by 3racha while the others will also often help with songwriting. 3racha has also won an award of "Best Creator" during the 2023 Asia Artist Awards.
